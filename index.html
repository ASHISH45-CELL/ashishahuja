About me

I don't just analyse deals.
I care about the people behind them.

I'm a venture scout at LevlUp Ventures, sourcing early-stage deals across consumer, fintech, and AI. But the title is the least interesting part — what drives me is the founder conversations, the whiteboard sessions, and the moment a thesis clicks.

My VC instincts were forged on the operator side. Two years at Friendzo's founder's office taught me how hard it is to build, and why the right capital partner changes everything. I wrote the decks, tracked the metrics, and helped close the seed round. Then I came back to the other side of the table.

Before all of that, I was at AIC Inspire, reviewing founders, drafting theses, and learning how investors spot potential before it becomes pattern-matched. That experience shaped how I see the world — always searching for signal in the noise.
